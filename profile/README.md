# DreamyCloud Studio

DreamyCloud Studio builds human-centered AI tools and reflective software systems. Our work is guided by a belief that technology should support agency, not replace it — and that good design begins with understanding how people actually live and think. We move slowly on purpose.

## Philosophy

- AI should be calm, transparent, and honest about its limitations
- Psychological safety and user agency are non-negotiable design constraints
- Long-term thinking takes precedence over short-term engagement
- Infrastructure should be humane — legible, maintainable, and kind to the people who depend on it
- Ethical considerations are not a layer applied at the end; they are part of the foundation

## Current Projects

**Polaris: Inner North**
An AI companion for navigating long-term personal goals through gentle structure. Polaris works alongside the user — not in front of them — helping surface clarity over time without pressure or optimization theater.

**Pawsport**
Tools supporting intentional pet relocation and logistics. Pawsport addresses the real complexity of moving animals across borders: documentation, timelines, emotional load, and practical coordination.

**Personal Finance Planning**
A working demonstration of calm, structured financial planning tooling. Focused on clarity and long-term perspective rather than dashboards and alerts.

**Project and Task Management**
A working demonstration of minimal, reflective task management. Designed to support focused work without adding cognitive overhead.
